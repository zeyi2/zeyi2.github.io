This is a list some of the animes & light novels I enjoyed.

The rules

  • The order has no particular meaning.
  • ショートアニメ won't be listed here.

The List

Title Year Description
OVERLORD 2015 A badass story where the "protagonists" die at the hands of the villains. The novel is excellent, while the anime is just OK by the standards of a picky reader.
新世紀エヴァンゲリオン 1995 I have to admit I didn't get it, but the ending music was great.
Angle Beats! 2010 I'm still waiting for the remaining parts of the galgame :(
灼眼のシャナ 2005 Who don't love a tsudere loli?
Bochii the rock 2022 It was the first time that I enjoyed anime about high school girls and bands.
電波女と青春男 2011 Towa Erio used to be my USB icon. And the theme song is also great.
氷菓 2001 "他说冰菓就是要变得坚强的意思，如果我不坚强，终有一日会连悲鸣都无法发出，那样的话，我就会活的像行尸走肉一般。"
Pani Poni Dash! 2005 I like it, though I can't comprehend it.
Lycoris Recoil 2022 The story may be underdeveloped, but hey, isn't it nice to watch special agents in JK?
Black Rock Shooter 2010 I always thought Hatsune Miku Project DIVA has a BRS outfit, seems I remembered it wrong as I found neither in Future Tone or MEGA 39's.
這いよれ! ニャル子さん 2009 A Cthulhu anime which has little connection to the actual Cthulhu concept.
犬とハサミは使いよう 2013 I like meta-writing.
Accel World 2012 I watched this after watching Sword Art Online, and it turns out to be one of my favourite animes.
ぎんがてつどうものがたり 2003 The main reason why I play Honkai:Starrail. LOL.
Charlotte 2015 I still get nostalgic when rewatching this.
Chainsaw Man 2022 The original manga was too famous to be taken to compare with the animation. Overall I still enjoy the feeling to have the voice as an addition.
Cyberpunk: Edgerunner 2022 The best tribute to the concept of 'Cyperpunk'.
86 ―エイティシックス― 2021 This has the most abusive cinema scene, which formed an exceptionally great storytelling. The theme is unified around what have done wrong should not make excuse for wrong doings happening. The director is keen at using speechless scenes to contribute to emotion, and the use of interlude music is fantastic.
Re:ゼロから始める異世界生活 2016 An anti-fantasy-world anime.
コードギアス 反逆のルルーシュ 2006 Needless to say, All Hail Lelouch!
Levius 2019 Different from the original comic which is more or less like magic the anime adaptation is more of a focus on the steam punk setting.
Paprika 2006 Marvellous.
推しの子 2023 The topic is controversy but the manga managed it well and the animation is a elevation by adding emotion through voice acting. Idols are made up from a perfect lie, in the hope that it will become truth.
時々ボソッとロシア語でデレる隣のアーリャさん 2024 感觉…….不如顶针.
NOeSIS 嘘を吐いた記憶の物語 2014 To be honest, the manga is just a replication of the vision novel. But it is still interesting to read.
Strawberry Panic! 2006 Here it is the most wonderful, most unrestrained, most selfish love, and the pursuer of it. The specific character design of heroine has a vibe of the doll from Bloodborne. I quite appreciate the simplicity on the numbers of instruments used in the soundtracks.
謎の彼女X 2012 Scissor? Sun Burn? Drip play? Almost sounds like something you would expect on e-hentai, but it is actually normal. I really like the deep voice line of Urabe, which is very unique among the TV anime heroines.
